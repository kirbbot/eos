Welcome

You have arrived at the Functional Demo for the Eos Community Connections Portal. This prototype gives users the chance to experience the look and feel of the Eos production site, what it will be like to interact with the platform, as well as insight into some of the features that will be available.

As of today, this interactive prototype is designed for the desktop only ~ a mobile friendly version is in the pipes and we thank you for your patience.

In times of change we provide a safe and creative place to:

  • Find and be YOU

    Wherever you are on your path, we honor you and we have the community and resources to meet you where you are.

  • Ground and Connect

    Find your safe port in the sotm by connecting with others that “get you”. You don’t have to figure it all out on your own.

  • Heal and Grow

    Learn from those that have been through it, while being supported by our community of service providers.

  • Give Back

    Share your story and/or resources to help others along their journey. A great way to pay it forward !

Navigating big life changes? We have your back.

Major life transitions - there's just no avoiding them! These big life events can rock our worlds and often bring uncertainty, fear, and change. They can be joyous occasions or unexpected challenges, and they sometimes come in waves that can feel overwhelming. We believe that all people - including you - are much stronger than they know, and we offer you perspective, hope, inspiration, and access to resources to help on your journey.

Want to talk with someone whose “been there before?” TILC Ambassadors are here for you.

Who are the ambassadors ?

You’ve risen from the ashes yourself, you know how hard it can be. Fulfill your role as a change agent by using your experiences to help others struggling with like challenges.

Membership Features

  • All members sign a code of conduct

    We agree to treat each other with respect and love, and we hold all members accountable.

  • System rating based on peer feedback

    Transparency means members can rate every interaction with each other and organizations.

  • Trusted Moderators

    Only members can post, and we keep things safe by making sure everyone’s playing nice..

  • Highly selective data sharing

    Share your story and/or resources to help others along their journey. A great way to pay it forward!
