Who can see your profile info

You can choose who sees what on your profile, like showing your phone number to only you Trust Tier 4 connections. Just go to your profile and click on the "Edit" button!

Who can see your content

You can choose who sees and interacts with your stories, threads and collections every time you create new content. To change the privacy setting of your past content, go to your profile, find the item you want to change, click on the privacy icon and select an audience

Who can message you

Manage muted users

Once you mute someone, you can't see any of their content, and won't get notified of any of their activities, including their stories, threads, comments, replies, and reactions. See a list of users you have muted.
